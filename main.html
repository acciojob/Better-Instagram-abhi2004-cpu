Better Instagram — Drag & Drop to Swap

Drag any tile and drop it on another tile to swap their images. (Each tile has id="div1" .. "div6" as requested.)

div1 — camera
div2 — flower
div3 — mountains
div4 — skyline
div5 — interior
div6 — road
Tip: you can also press Space/Enter while focused on a tile to pick it up, then press Space/Enter on another tile to swap (basic keyboard support).
